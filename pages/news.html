Spotlight: Foster House

This amazing residence is immersed in the sun-drenched nature of the Costa Brava and blends harmoniously into the surrounding landscape. The clean, geometric lines of the structure give a sense of solidity and robustness, while the interior is characterized by open spaces that follow one another, with large windows that blur the boundary between interior and exterior. The furniture is essential and underlines the beauty and purity of the materials used in the architectural envelope. Brutalist architecture is often associated with a feeling of austere elegance, and this project embodies this perfectly, enriched however by the warm Mediterranean atmosphere in which it is immersed.

2023 WAN Awards

The WAN Association awarded the 2023 Award of Excellence for New Construction to BKL Studio. The winning project, Glass Box, it is characterized by a permeability of the spaces.

Brutalism by the lake

This incredible brutalist architecture is characterized by its simple geometric shapes, a frank display of the raw materials used and a sense of solidity. The building has exposed concrete walls and pillars that form straight lines and sharp angles, with openings for natural light to filter through.
